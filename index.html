Our Process
Our process is a flexible framework that adapts and responds to your needs and user requirements to deliver the best possible product.
Step 1
Definition
The first step is to define the project scope with your team. We host a workshop to gather insights on your company, your product and your users.
Step 2
Design
Once the requirements are set, we prototype and test the product with real users. This will give us all the design assets to build the product.
Step 3
Development
Our engineers go to work developing the product with the most suitable and future-proof technology. Meanwhile, the entire team is engaged to add content and ensure quality.
Step 4
Support and Iterations
Once delivered, we offer support and further assistance to continually improve your product. From security updates to content and functionality optimisation.
